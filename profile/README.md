# The Vision

**As Above, So Within** bridges the ancient wisdom of Persian mythology with the infinite complexity of fractal mathematics. This walk-in sculpture invites visitors to step inside a living mathematical form—a three-dimensional Mandelbulb that echoes the legendary _Gaokerena_, the Tree of Life.

In Persian cosmology, the _Gaokerena_ stands at the center of the world, its branches reaching toward the heavens while its roots delve into the deepest waters. The divine _Simurgh_ bird nests in its crown, guardian of wisdom and renewal. This sculpture embodies that same duality: exterior complexity that gives way to an interior sanctuary.

The Mandelbulb formula—a three-dimensional extension of the Mandelbrot set—generates endlessly recursive patterns that mirror natural forms: clouds, mountains, the branches of trees. At Burning Man 2026's "Axis Mundi" theme, this piece becomes a literal axis point where participants can enter the infinite, crossing the threshold between mathematical abstraction and embodied experience.
